What I do

Analytics with a sales floor behind it

I spent five years selling windows, doors, and cabinets to US contractors, working CRM pipelines, quotes, and follow-up cadences. That means I read a retention query the way an operator does: not "97% churn" but "which customers do we call, on which day, with what offer." My analysis ends in a recommendation someone can act on, or it is not finished.

My toolkit covers SQL (MySQL), Power BI with DAX and star schema modeling, Tableau, and Excel through Power Query and Power Pivot. Each project below is packaged the way a stakeholder would receive it: case study, executive summary, presentation deck, and documented code. Where the data cannot support a conclusion, the case study says so.
